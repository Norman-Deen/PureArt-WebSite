Product

For many e-retailers, product images play a decisive role at the moment of purchase. The most fundamental quality of a product image is that it presents the product in an attractive way. Therefore, it is important that it conveys information that is accurate and relevant. A good product image should be adaptable. Looking at the bigger picture, your product image obviously has more purposes than just selling the product in an appealing and honest way in the webshop. In most cases, you also want to be able to use it in social media, catalogs, email campaigns, and more. In summary Creating truly great product images is not only about what the image expresses and how it fits into its intended context — it is also a matter of usability and scalability.
