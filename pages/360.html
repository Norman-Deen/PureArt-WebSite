360°- AR

With a 360 feature, you can create a realistic product that can be rotated and turned to view it from every possible angle.

You can also create ready-made buttons to easily visualize how the product’s color can be changed.

You can even add functions to show how things move — for example, a drawer that opens.

By creating an AR file, you can produce an object that allows the customer to place the product in their room and see in real time how it would look in their home.
